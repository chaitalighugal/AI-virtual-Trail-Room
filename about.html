Our Story

VirtualFit was born from a simple frustration: the uncertainty of buying clothes online. Our founders, a team of fashion enthusiasts and technology experts, experienced countless disappointments with ill-fitting online purchases.

In 2020, we set out to solve this problem using cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning technologies. After years of research and development, we created a platform that accurately simulates how clothes look and fit on individual body types.

Today, VirtualFit serves millions of customers worldwide, helping them make confident fashion choices from the comfort of their homes.
